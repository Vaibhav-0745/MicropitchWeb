Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, profile picture, etc.
  • Business Content: Videos, pitch titles, descriptions, and related metadata.
  • Usage Data: Device info, IP address, browser type, app interactions.
  • Location Data: If you allow location access.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information to:

  • Display your startup pitches and allow sharing with other users.
  • Improve app features and user experience.
  • Respond to feedback or support requests.
  • Personalize your feed or notifications.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. How We Protect Your Information

  • All data is stored securely using industry standards.
  • Passwords are encrypted.
  • We do not sell or trade your personal information.

4. Sharing of Information

We may share information only:

  • With trusted partners to help operate the app (e.g. hosting providers).
  • When required by law.
  • With your consent, for features like "featured pitch of the week."

5. Third-Party Services

Our platform may use third-party services (e.g. YouTube, Firebase, analytics tools). These have their own privacy policies.

6. Children's Privacy

MicroPitch is not intended for users under the age of 16.

7. Your Choices

  • You may edit or delete your profile at any time.
  • You may request your data to be removed by emailing us.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: micropitch2025@gmail.com
